# T46 — Hackathon product team assignment

**Phase**: 5 — Partner and hackathon management
**Area**: Hackathon, Team
**Requirements**: L1-005, L1-006, L2-024
**Source**: Screen-Level Missing Inventory — "Product builder assignment"

## Goal

Allow assignment of team members as builders on a product record.

## Scope

- Builder picker integrated in the product create/edit dialog (T45).
- Searchable list of team members; multi-select with avatars.
- Display on the product card: avatar stack with overflow count.
- Remove-builder action with confirmation snackbar.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Picker designed with loading/empty/error states.
- [ ] Builder display on product card designed.
- [ ] Mobile variant per T21.
