# T35 — Partner stage history

**Status**: Accepted
**Phase**: 5 — Partner and hackathon management
**Area**: Partner
**Requirements**: L1-004, L2-017
**Source**: Screen-Level Missing Inventory — "Partner stage history"

## Goal

Show a chronological audit of partner stage changes on partner detail.

## Scope

- Timeline component below partner notes (or in a tab).
- Each entry: from-stage → to-stage, actor, timestamp, optional note.
- Empty state when no transitions yet.
- Real-time append on stage change (links to T13/T54 for live update treatment).

- [ ] Timeline component designed with at least three entries.
