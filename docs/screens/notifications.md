# Notifications

Notification center page across loading, empty, populated and error states.

## How to navigate

1. Sign in (see [sign-in](sign-in.md)).
2. Click the **bell** icon in the top app bar (or **Notifications** in the side nav) → `/notifications` (`G2f4C` / `g74830` / `F0iAlY`).
3. To reach the variant states:
   - **Loading** (`UDKRi`) — observed during the initial fetch.
   - **Empty** (`J2gC1T` / `nS0Dv`) — no notifications exist for the user.
   - **Error** (`x6ykzA`) — fetch fails.
