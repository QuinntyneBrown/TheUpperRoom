# Dialog: Partner Stage — Advance

Confirmation when advancing a partner forward through the funnel stages.

## How to navigate

1. Sign in (see [sign-in](sign-in.md)).
2. Click **Partners** → click a partner row → partner detail page.
3. In the stage stepper, click a stage **after** the current stage → opens the confirmation dialog (`Od23Q`).
