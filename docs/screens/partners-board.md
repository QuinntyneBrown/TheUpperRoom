# Partners Board

Kanban-style stage board for partners with drag-and-drop and per-stage states.

## How to navigate

1. Sign in (see [sign-in](sign-in.md)).
2. Click **Partners** in the side nav.
3. Switch the view toggle from **List** to **Board** (or open `/partners/board`) → `QYgpt` / `DuvN0` / `MR62e`.
4. To reach the variant states:
   - **Drag In Progress** (`XKKcX`) — press-and-drag a card between columns.
   - **Empty Stage** (`lf82m`) — scroll to a stage column with no partners.
   - **Move Stage Sheet** (`S2GPyK`) — on mobile, tap the move-stage action on a card.
