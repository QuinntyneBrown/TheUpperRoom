# Dialog: Partner Stage — Revert (Backward)

Confirmation when moving a partner backward in the funnel.

## How to navigate

1. Sign in (see [sign-in](sign-in.md)).
2. Click **Partners** → click a partner row → partner detail page.
3. In the stage stepper, click a stage **before** the current stage → opens the revert confirmation dialog (`S76ttD`).
