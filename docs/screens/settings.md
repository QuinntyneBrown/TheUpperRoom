# Settings

Settings index plus profile sub-page.

## How to navigate

1. Sign in (see [sign-in](sign-in.md)).
2. Open the [profile menu](profile-menu.md) and click **Settings** (or click **Settings** in the side nav) → `/settings` (`i4gsOz` / `aaFul` / `LV0y4`).
3. To reach the **Profile** sub-page (`F3gf8p`), tap **Profile** from the mobile settings index.
