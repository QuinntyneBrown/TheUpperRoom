# Dialog: New Contact From Partner

Variant of the new-contact dialog launched from a partner detail page; created contact is auto-linked to the partner.

## How to navigate

1. Sign in (see [sign-in](sign-in.md)).
2. Click **Partners** in the side nav.
3. Click any partner row → partner detail page.
4. Click **Add Contact** (or the equivalent action on the partner contacts panel) → opens the dialog (`W4ieIT` / `xIjMH`).
5. After save, the partner detail returns showing the **Created and Linked Toast** (see [partner-detail](partner-detail.md)).
