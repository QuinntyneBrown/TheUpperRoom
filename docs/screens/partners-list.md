# Partners (List)

Tabular partner list view (board view is in [partners-board](partners-board.md)).

## How to navigate

1. Sign in (see [sign-in](sign-in.md)).
2. Click **Partners** in the side nav → `/partners` (`M1XBkr` / `X064R` / `Nc08c`).
3. To reach the variant:
   - **Deleted Toast** (`WgZjz`) — delete a partner from the partner detail page (see [partner delete dialog](dialog-partner-delete-confirmation.md)); the list returns with the toast.
