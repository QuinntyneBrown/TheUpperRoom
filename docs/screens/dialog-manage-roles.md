# Dialog: Manage Roles

Assign roles to a team member (mobile bottom sheet variant).

## How to navigate

1. Sign in (see [sign-in](sign-in.md)) as a team admin on a mobile viewport.
2. Click **Team** in the side nav (see [team](team.md)).
3. Tap a team member's overflow / manage action → in the manage sheet, tap **Manage roles** → opens the bottom sheet (`GAxsg`).
